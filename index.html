Hair Care Routines

Daily Care

Gentle brushing, minimal heat styling, and moisturizing shampoo to maintain healthy hair oils.

Weekly Care

Deep conditioning masks, scalp massages, and limited washing prevent dryness and breakage.

Seasonal Care

Adjust routines according to weather: hydration in summer, nourishment in winter.

DIY Remedies

Dry Hair

Coconut oil + honey mask weekly deeply hydrates and restores softness.

Hair Fall

Onion juice + aloe vera strengthens roots and reduces hair shedding effectively.

Dandruff

Mix yogurt + fenugreek paste, massage into scalp for 30 mins to fight flakes.

Recommended Products

Shampoos

Sulfate-free shampoos like OGX, L’Oréal Botanicals, or Khadi Herbal maintain hair health.

Conditioners

Nourishing conditioners suited for your hair type help maintain shine and manageability.

Serums & Oils

Argan oil, castor oil, and lightweight serums control frizz and promote hair growth.
